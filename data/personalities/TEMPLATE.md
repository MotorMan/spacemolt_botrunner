## Tips for Good Bot Personalities:

1. **Be specific**: Instead of "friendly", try "greets everyone with enthusiasm and remembers their names"

2. **Give them quirks**: Maybe they always talk about mining, or they're obsessed with fuel efficiency, or they love exploring dangerous systems

3. **Define their expertise**: Are they a veteran trader? A newbie miner? A crafty manufacturer? This affects what they know and talk about

4. **Set the tone**: Formal? Casual? Sarcastoic? Motherly? Robotic? Enthusiastic?

5. **Add catchphrases or habits**: "Well I'll be!", "Space dust and stars!", always ends messages with an emoji, etc.

6. **Keep it concise**: The LLM will use this as a system prompt, so clear and direct works best

## File Naming:

The system looks for personality files in this order:
1. `Bot Name.md` (exact name with spaces)
2. `bot_name.md` (lowercase with underscores)
3. `Bot_Name.md` (capitalized with underscores)

So for a bot named "Hannah Hollo", any of these work:
- `Hannah Hollo.md`
- `hannah_hollo.md`
- `Hannah_Hollo.md`
